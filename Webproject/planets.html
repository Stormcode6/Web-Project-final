Planets

The game takes on a number of planets and moons throughout the solar system

Earth

There are two areas on Earth. The Cosmodrome in Old Russia and the Euopean Dead Zone. the Cosmodrome is a old space port where starships left to explore and colonize other worlds the EDZ is a area around where a shard of the the Traveller crashed into the ground. It is corupted by the supernatual energy emited

The Moon

The Moon has one area in the Sea of Storms. it is a Massive underground fortress created by the Hive and is older than human civilisation.

Nessus

Nessus is a dwarf planet which as been turned into a Vex facility nearly all the way to it core

Europa

Europa is the location of Bray Exoscience where experimets on humans were preformed as well as Riis Reborn, a city built by the Fallen

Dreaming City

The Dreamming City is a city created with the power of a wish by The Awoken at its centre lies Riven of a Thousand Voices, the last Ahmakara. A shapeshifting dragon capable of granting wishes.
